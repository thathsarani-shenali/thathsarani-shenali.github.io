Awards & Achievements

Champions - RevolUX 2022

RevolUX 2.0 is a designathon organized by AIESEC at the University of Colombo in partnership with the IEEE student branch of UCSC. It aims to provide undergraduate students with an opportunity to showcase their creativity and artistic skills by developing innovative designs. The event was preceded by three workshops and two rounds of competition. Our team consisted of Mino Gallawatta, Dinithi Weerakoon, and myself. In the first round, we made it to the top 10 teams and advanced to the final competition. During the 24-hour challenge, we had to present a solution to a given problem statement. finally, we were lucky enough to emerge champions of this RevolUX designathon.
